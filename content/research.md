_Emergent models & human collaboration_

# Building powerful and practical AI for scientific discovery.

## Foundation models for biomedicine
I train general-purpose models that can transfer knowledge across biomedical modalities and stay reliable under distribution shifts. I am especially interested in emergent behaviors that appear when these models reason across experiments, molecules, and patient data.

## Scientist-aligned interfaces
Models must converse with scientists, so I design systems that expose their reasoning, ground explanations in domain knowledge, and invite collaboration. My work aims to make interpretability, hypothesis generation, and practical lab workflows feel tightly integrated.
